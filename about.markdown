I grew up in a small town in Vermont and which gave me a profound appreciation for time in the outdoors. When I'm not in the office, adventures in the outdoors keep me refreshed and excited! 

I raced for Colby College, a Division 1 collegiate team, in central Maine for four years, which included two top-30 finishes at NCAAs, and two podium finishes at Junior Nationals. Results aside, 15 years of competitive skiing taught me how to enjoy being outside, no matter the weather!
